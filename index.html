About SEM

It refers to different types of strategies and techniques that can be used to generate visitor traffic in huge numbers from SERPS. Its goal is to enhance site visibility. This is achieved by using organic search rankings, contextual advertising and paid search. It is rather a broad term to include Pay-Per-Click (PPC) and Search Engine Optimization (SEO). It involves total site or brand presence in search engines like Google.

The Search Engine Marketing Process You Need

Here’s the SEM procedure you’ll undergo as we create the most result-oriented strategy for you, to make your brand create a spot in the heart of clients.
Perform a Keyword Search Look for a keyword that strategically applies to your business or product. Common, high-profile keywords may invite higher bids, while more obscure ones may offer less competition. So, the first step here is to carry out a search for all the words and phrases that are more relevant to your company or the service you offer.
Choose a Relevant Keyword Pick keywords that are strategically linked to your business and SEM approach. So, you’ll have to know which phrases to prioritize. It’s also crucial to distinguish between implicit and explicit keywords. As the top SEM agency in Dubai, we help you choose the most relevant options based on your local context and overall strategy.
Make Ads that will appear in the SERPs Next, we’ll help you craft an ad that speaks clearly to your target audience. Your ad may include catchy visuals, a strong CTA, or a display of your company’s best features. Our expert team of marketers, designers, and writers will craft a unique ad that shows your company in the most alluring light.
Decide on a Bidding Strategy based on PPC
You’ll have to outbid other advertisers fighting for the same keywords on the search engine. Excessive bidding may guarantee you a spot but increase your costs without conversions. Under-bidding pushes your competitors to the top spots on the SERPs.

SEO or PPC

You may want to know which one to adopt to promote your online business – SEO or PPC techniques! The fact is both are worth the effort and try. Hence, you should adopt them both as it can help your business to flourish quickly than what is achieved using a single technique. To derive fast results, PPC is a great choice while SEO can offer long-term growth. The knowledgeable search engine marketing agency Dubai can guide you to choose the most appropriate one suited to your circumstances and needs.

Why SEM is vital for Advertisements?

Not considering PPC for online business promotion simply means missing out on something crucial. PPC offers sites with sustainable traffic against some payment. Users might prefer to click organic listings that are highly placed when compared to advertisements. However, relevant keywords should be selected wisely for PPC campaigns to stay within the budget.

Why invest in PPC?

The leading search engine marketing agency Dubai suggest PPC campaigns as paid search results tend to dominate above-the-fold content. On mobile, you will be able to find three ads while on desktop with four ads. This means, your targeted audience will be able to view the paid ads without fail before checking organic search results. Besides this, good SEM is sure to help your brand occupy both the spots.
